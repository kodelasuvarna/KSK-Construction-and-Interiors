Founder And CEO​​

Kota Shyam Kumar, a Master of Design and Construction Management, founded KSK Construction & Interior Company with a vision to revolutionize the Indian construction industry. His expertise has driven the company's growth and secured prestigious projects across the country. KSK Construction Company has gained recognition for its exceptional craftsmanship and innovative construction. Implementing innovative techniques and delivering exceptional results for every project.​

In addition to our expertise in construction, KSK Construction & Interior Company also specializes in interior design. Our team of skilled interior designers creates stunning spaces that combine functionality and aesthetics, tailored to meet the unique needs and preferences of our clients. With a keen eye for detail and a commitment to delivering excellence, we transform spaces into beautiful and functional environments that leave a lasting impression.​​
